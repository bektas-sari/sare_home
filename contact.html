Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a property viewing by calling us directly, filling out our contact form, or clicking the "Schedule Viewing" button on any property listing. We'll coordinate a convenient time for you.

What documents do I need to buy a property?

Typically, you'll need proof of income, bank statements, pre-approval letter from a lender, identification, and proof of funds for down payment. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements.

Do you offer property management services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive property management services including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and regular property inspections.

How long does the buying process take?

The buying process typically takes 30-45 days from offer acceptance to closing, depending on financing, inspections, and other factors. We'll keep you informed throughout the process.
